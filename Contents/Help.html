Browser help for Windows:

If you are having problems viewing the presentations or you see a warning on your web browser such as the one below that says :

"To help protect your security, Internet Explorer has restricted this file from showing active content that could access your computer. Click here for options..."

To correct this problem, click the yellow bar and select Allow Blocked Content. Then select Yes in the Security Warning window. See image below for a sample.

 

This allows blocked content only for the video files and does not affect other Windows security features. You may have to repeat this step for every video file you open in windows.

Play videos on iTunes/iPod/iPad/iPhone:

Plug your video capable iPod into your computer and launch iTunes. If you do not have iTunes installed, go to the Apple website to download iTunes.

To view presentations in a video capable iPod, iPhone or iPad: Transfer the videos to iTunes by launching iTunes first. Go to the "File" menu, in Ttunes and select " Add Folder to Library...". Navigate to “My Computer,” open this drive and selec the" Video" folder. Itunes will start importing the files to your Movies library. To import files on the Mac, you will need to select "Add to library..." on the iTunes "File" menu and navigate to the Video folder on this drive to select it.

When iTunes has finished transferring the files go back to the "Movies" icon in iTunes, click on the "Home Videos" tab to start viewing the videos on your computer. To Sync your iPod, iPhone or iPad please go to the Apple tutorials page at http://www.apple.com/support/itunes/
